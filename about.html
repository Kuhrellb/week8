Michael Jordan played in his original shoe line of Air Jordan 1-14. Each shoe was released in multiple retro color ways. There were over 50 releases of these shoes over the course of his playing carrer. Even after his retirment and to this day he continues to release new colorways and retro style colorways from his brand.
